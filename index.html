FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Which platforms does BlinkFlow support?
+
BlinkFlow is built exclusively for Meesho sellers. We go deep on Meesho's exact settlement structure, CSV formats, and fee system. Amazon and Flipkart support is planned for 2027.
How does BlinkFlow handle the settlement delay problem?
+
You upload both this month's and next month's settlement file. BlinkFlow matches every Order ID across both files automatically. Orders not yet settled are shown as pending with an estimated value and expected date.
Is my data safe and private?
+
Yes. Your data is encrypted and stored securely on Supabase servers. We never share or sell your business data. Each account's data is completely isolated.
Do I need to install anything?
+
No installation needed. BlinkFlow is a web app that works in any browser on mobile or desktop. Just upload your files and your P&L is ready.
How accurate is the profit calculation?
+
Very accurate. We built and tested the engine against real Meesho data from multiple seller accounts. It accounts for TCS, TDS, return shipping (₹162), RTO costs, multi-quantity orders, and ad spend deductions.
Can I manage multiple Meesho accounts?
+
Yes. The Pro plan supports up to 5 accounts. The Agency plan supports unlimited accounts with a combined dashboard view — perfect for account managers who handle multiple sellers.
